## 📌 Project Overview
This project is part of the Digital Literacy course, designed to help students understand and apply essential digital skills in real-world scenarios. As a “Student Digital Ambassador,” the objective was to explore how digital tools, platforms, and communication methods can be used effectively, responsibly, and professionally.

The project consists of five structured tasks, each focusing on a specific aspect of digital literacy such as awareness, digital presence, communication, and cybersecurity. All the work has been organized into this repository to form a complete digital portfolio.

---

## 🎯 Project Theme
**Digital Survival Guide for Freshers**

This project follows a central theme aimed at helping first-year students understand how to navigate the digital world safely and efficiently. Each task contributes to building a step-by-step understanding of how a student can develop digital awareness, avoid common mistakes, and build a strong online presence from the beginning of their college journey.

## 📂 Task Details

### 🔹 Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
In this task, I created a one-page infographic using Canva based on the theme “Digital Survival Guide for Freshers.” The infographic highlights key areas such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools, building a professional online presence, and maintaining proper email etiquette. This task helped in understanding how to present information visually in a clear and engaging way.

---

### 🔹 Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
For this task, I created and updated my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. The goal was to establish a basic professional presence online. GitHub was used to showcase coding-related work, LinkedIn for professional networking, and Kaggle for exploring data science and problem-solving. This task emphasized the importance of building a digital identity early.

---

### 🔹 Task 3 – Platforms Exploration
In this task, I explored both coding and collaboration platforms. I solved a beginner-level problem on HackerRank to understand how coding platforms function. Additionally, I created a Google Form titled **“Digital Survival Quiz for Freshers”** consisting of five questions to test awareness of digital literacy concepts.

👉 Google Form Link: *https://forms.gle/3eFnXPmgrRjLNTsJ8*

This task helped me understand how digital tools can be used for both learning and interaction.

---

### 🔹 Task 4 – Email Etiquette
This task focused on professional communication. I wrote two formal emails — one requesting an assignment extension and another applying for an internship opportunity. Along with this, I created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts to promote responsible online behavior among students. This task highlighted the importance of tone, clarity, and structure in digital communication.

---

### 🔹 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
In this task, I studied cybercrime by creating a case study on UPI payment fraud. The case study explains how such frauds occur, who is targeted, and their consequences. I also created a prevention checklist with practical tips to help students stay safe online. This task made me more aware of common digital risks and how they can be avoided.
