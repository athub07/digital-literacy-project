## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a trusted source.  
- Remember that you do not need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.  
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious payment links sent through messages or social media.  
- Always verify the identity of the person before making or accepting any payment.  
- Do not scan random QR codes, especially if you are unsure about the source.  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi while making online payments or accessing banking apps.  
- Regularly check your bank transactions and enable alerts for all payments.  
- If you suspect any fraud, immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in).  
- You can also call the Cyber Crime Helpline number 1930 for immediate assistance.
