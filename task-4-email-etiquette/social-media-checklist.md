Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

Do’s
- Keep your profile clean and professional, especially on platforms like LinkedIn.  
- Think before posting anything, as it can create a long-term digital impression.  
- Use privacy settings wisely to control who can see your content.  
- Share useful and positive content that reflects your interests and personality.  
- Be respectful while interacting with others online, even during disagreements.  

Don’ts
- Don’t share sensitive personal information like phone number, address, or financial details.  
- Don’t post offensive, inappropriate, or controversial content that could harm your reputation.  
- Don’t believe or forward unverified information without checking its authenticity.  
- Don’t engage in online arguments or toxic discussions unnecessarily.  
- Don’t use informal or careless language on professional platforms.
